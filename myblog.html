BOOK REVIEWS:

Recently, I've finished reading The PowderMage Trilogy. Here's a review of the series:

Book Series: Powder Mage Trilogy
Author: Brian McClellan
Genre: Flintlock Fantasy
Topics: Magic, Military Coup & Politics, Racism, Polytheism.

Review: The trilogy held me spellbound from start to finish. It was nearly impossible to put the books down; whenever I did, I was dying of anticipation the whole time. McClellan skillfully blends the elements of flintlock fantasy, seamlessly intertwining gunpowder and magic to create a unique and exciting world. The synergy between gunpowder and magic in this trilogy is an amazing take on the fantasy genre. Both these elements go really well with each other, featuring captivating characters and their complex relationships that blend seamlessly with the overarching plot. All the intricate political intricacies and character relations make it even better by keeping the readers engaged throughout.
